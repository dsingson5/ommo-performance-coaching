02 — THE RACE

Anatomy of a Hyrox.

Tap any station to see the full standards, equipment, and weights. 1 km of running between every station.

8Stations
1.0KM between
∞Mental reps
— 01 —
Ski Erg
1,000 m
EQUIPConcept2 SkiErg
STATION DETAILS
1,000m on the SkiErg. Standing position, both poles pulled simultaneously. Hip-hinge driven, core-engaged. Same machine used by Olympic Nordic skiers.
— 02 —
Sled Push
50 m
M152 kg  W102 kg
STATION DETAILS
50m sled push. Total weight (sled + plates): 152kg for Men's Open, 102kg for Women's Open. Pro division loads are heavier. Full extension required, no stutter steps allowed.
— 03 —
Sled Pull
50 m
M103 kg  W78 kg
STATION DETAILS
50m sled pull, hand-over-hand. 103kg for Men's Open, 78kg for Women's Open. Pull rope into a designated box, then walk back to reposition sled. Two rope-loads required.
— 04 —
Burpee Broad Jumps
80 m
STDChest to floor · Two-foot jump
STATION DETAILS
80m of burpee broad jumps. Chest must touch the floor at the bottom; both feet must leave and land together on the broad jump. The most cardiovascularly demanding station.
— 05 —
Rowing
1,000 m
EQUIPConcept2 RowErg
STATION DETAILS
1,000m row on Concept2. Damper set by athlete preference. The "recovery" station — most athletes use it to bring heart rate down while still banking meters.
— 06 —
Farmers Carry
200 m
M2×24 kg  W2×16 kg
STATION DETAILS
200m carrying two kettlebells. 2×24kg for Men's Open, 2×16kg for Women's Open. Kettlebells must remain off the ground for the full carry — drops require restart from that point.
— 07 —
Sandbag Lunges
100 m
M20 kg  W10 kg
STATION DETAILS
100m walking lunges with sandbag on shoulders. 20kg Men's Open, 10kg Women's Open. Back knee must touch the floor; sandbag stays on shoulders the entire 100m.
— 08 —
Wall Balls
75 / 100 reps
M6 kg @ 10ft  W4 kg @ 9ft
STATION DETAILS
100 reps Men's Open, 75 reps Women's Open. 6kg ball to 10ft target (M), 4kg to 9ft (W). Full squat below parallel, ball must hit at or above the target line. Final station — and the one that ends most races.
— RUN INTERVALS —
8 × 1 km runs between every station — the difference between a finish and a PR.
